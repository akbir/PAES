Would you rather hear the louche pun drawn
from glory hole, lip wrap or fingering
or hear a tiny hammer striking wire? Would you rather see the molten birthing glass?
Seat Eros next to Kronos, for the banter. I heard she's yet unplowed -- I heard your quiver dangled down -- 
I heard you dwell in borrowed forms -- love's nothing but glimmer-to-wither, dawn's fireflies expired.
In this place we sift & bounce the words like dice thrice dip a pipe into the magma, o my stars.
